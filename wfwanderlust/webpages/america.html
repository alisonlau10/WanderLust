Attractions in Phoenix and Monterey Bay

Attractions in Phoenix, Arizona

Desert Botanical Garden

This beautiful garden showcases a wide variety of desert plants from around the world. It's a great place to learn about the unique flora and fauna of the Sonoran Desert.

Heard Museum

The Heard Museum is dedicated to Native American art and culture, featuring an extensive collection of art, artifacts, and exhibits that highlight the rich history and traditions of Native American tribes.

Camelback Mountain

A popular hiking spot, Camelback Mountain offers stunning panoramic views of Phoenix and the surrounding desert landscape. It's a challenging hike, but the breathtaking vistas make it worth the effort.

Phoenix Art Museum

The largest art museum in the southwestern United States, the Phoenix Art Museum features a diverse collection of art from different cultures and time periods, including American, Asian, European, and Latin American art.

Attractions in Monterey Bay, California

Monterey Bay Aquarium

One of the most renowned aquariums in the world, the Monterey Bay Aquarium is located right on the waterfront. It offers an incredible variety of marine life exhibits, including sea otters, sharks, penguins, and jellyfish.

Cannery Row

Made famous by John Steinbeck's novel, Cannery Row is a vibrant waterfront street lined with shops, restaurants, and galleries. It's a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.

17-Mile Drive

This scenic drive along the Monterey Peninsula offers breathtaking views of the coastline, golf courses, and iconic landmarks like the Lone Cypress tree and Pebble Beach Golf Links.

Point Lobos State Natural Reserve

Located just south of Monterey, Point Lobos is a stunning nature reserve with hiking trails, picturesque coves, and diverse wildlife. It's a great spot for nature enthusiasts and photographers.
