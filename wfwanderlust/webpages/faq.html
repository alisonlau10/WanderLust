FAQs

How do I navigate this site?

Click on the panels in the section labeled "Countries" above. You will be taken to a page with information on recommended attractions & living accomadations.

How do I know if this website is reliable?

We will try our best to provide you with the latest information. If you notice any misleading information, please email us at wfwanderlust@gmail.com.

Are you travel experts?

No, we aren't travel experts. We are a group of high school students who are interested in traveling and would like to gather resources on budget traveling.

Is travel insurance important?

Yes! A travel insurance plan can get you covered for medical emergencies & other accidents. We recommend using insuremytrip or TravelInsurance.com to help find a plan that matches your needs.

How do I stay safe while traveling?

A few tips include avoid posting on social media, making copies of important documents, buying travel insurance & watching your valuables. For more information, please visit 6 Essential Travel Tips or 25 Important Travel Safety Tips You Need To Know.
