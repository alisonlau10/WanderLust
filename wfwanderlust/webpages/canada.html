Attractions

Niagra Falls

Location: Ontario, Canada

Price: Free

Overview: Niagara Falls is located on the border between Canada and the United States. It is made up of three waterfalls: Horseshoe Falls, American Falls, and Bridal Veil Falls. The waterfalls are located on the Niagara River, which connects Lake Erie and Lake Ontario. Niagara Falls is one of the most popular tourist destinations in North America, attracting millions of visitors each year. The best time to visit Niagara Falls is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and the falls are at their fullest. There are many activities to do in Niagara Falls, including boat tours, hiking trails, and casinos.

For more information, please visit Niagra Falls

Notre Dame Basilica

Location: Montreal, Quebec

Price: $13-15

Overview: Notre-Dame Basilica is a popular tourist attraction in Montreal, Canada, and a stunning example of Gothic Revival architecture. The basilica is open every day from 8am to 4:30pm, except during religious services, and visitors are charged an admission fee that varies depending on the time of day and whether or not they opt for a guided tour. As a place of worship, the basilica has a dress code that visitors are expected to follow, and flash photography is not permitted during religious services. Guided tours are available in several languages and offer a more in-depth look at the building's history and architecture. The basilica is accessible to visitors with mobility issues, with a ramp available at the entrance and an elevator to access the choir loft, which is only accessible during guided tours.

For more information, please visit Notre Dame Basilica

Butchart Gardens

Location: Brentwood Bay, British Columbia

Price: Varies from $25.50-39.50 depending on the season.

Overview: Butchart Gardens is a world-renowned garden located near Brentwood Bay, British Columbia, Canada. Visitors are advised to check the hours of operation and admission fees before visiting. There is no specific dress code, but comfortable shoes are recommended. Photography is allowed, but commercial photography and the use of tripods or other large equipment requires prior permission. Guided tours are available in several languages and offer a more in-depth look at the plants and flowers. The gardens are accessible to visitors with mobility issues, with paved pathways and ramps throughout the grounds. Wheelchairs and electric scooters are available for rent on a first-come, first-served basis.

For more information, please visit Butchart Gardens

Ramada by Wyndham Niagara Falls

Location: Niagara Falls, Ontario

Price: Varies, around $93-140

Ramada by Wyndham Niagara Falls is a hotel located in the heart of Niagara Falls, Ontario, Canada. It's just a short walk from the famous Niagara Falls and a few blocks from Clifton Hill, a popular tourist district with many attractions. The hotel is also located just a short drive from the Niagara Fallsview Casino and the Skylon Tower, which offers stunning views of the falls and the surrounding area. Other nearby attractions include Niagara-on-the-Lake, a charming town known for its wineries and historic sites, and the Niagara Parks Botanical Gardens, which features a variety of gardens and a butterfly conservatory. There's plenty to see and do near Ramada by Wyndham Niagara Falls!

Amenities: Smoke-free hotel, free wifi, RV parking, shuttle to local attractions, and laundry services.

For more information, please visit Ramada by Wyndham Niagara Falls.

Hotel Plaza Quebec

Location: Quebec, Canada

Price: Varies, around $120-180

Hotel Plaza Quebec is located in the Sainte-Foy-Sillery-Cap-Rouge district of Quebec City. This district is known for its beautiful parks, historic sites, and excellent shopping and dining options. Some popular attractions near the hotel include the Aquarium du Quebec, which features a variety of marine life exhibits, and the Parc de la Plage-Jacques-Cartier, which offers beautiful views of the Saint Lawrence River. Other nearby attractions include the Musee National des Beaux-Arts du Quebec, which features a collection of Quebecois art, and the Place Laurier shopping center, which features a variety of stores and restaurants.

Amenities: Tropical garden, room service, playrooms, free wifi, indoor swimming pool, and gym.

For more information, please visit Hotel Plaza Quebec.
